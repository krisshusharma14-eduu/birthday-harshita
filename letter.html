A Letter To My Soul Sister 💌

The last page... but definitely not the end of our story.

Dear Harshita, 💜

Happy 20th Birthday, Harshita! 🎂💜 It's funny how life works. Back in December 2022, we met through people who are no longer a part of our lives, yet somehow that moment became one of the biggest blessings of my life because it brought me to you. At first, we were just two people getting to know each other. But slowly, without even realizing it, you became my comfort, my happiness, my biggest supporter, my safe place, and the sister I never had. Somewhere along the way, you stopped being just my best friend and became my family. Thank you for every laugh we've shared, every random conversation, every late-night talk, every hug, every piece of advice, every silly memory, and every moment you stood beside me without me even asking. Thank you for protecting me when I needed someone, comforting me during my hard times, believing in me when I doubted myself, and always reminding me that I'm never alone. I honestly don't know how to thank you for everything you've done for me. No words will ever be enough to express how grateful I am to have someone as kind, genuine, and caring as you in my life. You've made my life brighter in so many ways that you probably don't even realize. You are my favourite person, Harshita. You always have been, and you always will be. No matter how many new people come into our lives or how much time passes, you'll always be my number one. Our friendship is one of the most precious gifts I've ever received, and I'll treasure it forever. I have just one little request... please don't ever leave me. I can't imagine my life without our endless conversations, our laughter, our silly jokes, our random plans, and your constant support. Promise me that no matter how busy life gets or where life takes us, we'll always find our way back to each other because I never want to lose what we have. Life will change, we'll grow older, and we'll have different responsibilities, but I hope one thing never changes—our friendship. I want us to celebrate countless birthdays together, support each other through every success and every difficult moment, laugh until our stomachs hurt, and keep creating memories that we'll look back on years from now. Thank you for accepting me exactly as I am. Thank you for choosing me as your best friend. Thank you for making ordinary days feel extraordinary just by being there. Thank you for becoming my soul sister. On your 20th birthday, I pray that every dream in your heart comes true. May your life always be filled with happiness, success, peace, good health, beautiful surprises, and endless reasons to smile because you truly deserve the very best that life has to offer. Always remember this, no matter what happens in life, you'll never have to face anything alone because you'll always have me standing beside you, cheering for you, believing in you, and loving you. Happy 20th Birthday once again, my soul sister. Thank you for being my safe place, my favourite person, my biggest blessing, and the most beautiful friendship I could ever ask for. I love you more than words could ever express, and I always will. 🤍

With all my love, always ❤️

Krisshu 🌸

P.S. Thank you for being the sister life forgot to give me but somehow gifted me anyway. Please stay by my side forever. No matter where life takes us, you'll always have a home in my heart. 🤍

🎉 One Last Thing... 🎉

This website may have reached its last page... But our story never will. Here's to a lifetime of laughter, late-night talks, endless memories, and celebrating every birthday together. Happy Birthday, my forever favourite person. 💜🌸
